About Us

MedStop: One stop for all your medical needs

MedStop is an online booking webApp to book an emergency ambulance,buy medicines online, to transport the patient to a nearest hospital in the golden hour with just a click of a button. Our objective is to save lives and improve quality of healthcare services. At MedStop we provide a platform to help you find health solutions that are convenient and useful enough to continue for a longer time. Till date, we have had numerous users who have expressed gratitude and appreciation on our social media platforms for getting timely medical help. They were able to connect to the right doctors at affordable fees. Med-Stop delivers an all-in-one medical services website, in where anything you need, ranging from ambulance emergency booking, medicines, home help nurses to global discussion forums, everything will be just a click away. With Med-Stop you can be sure to receive not only more accessible, but also more affordable medicines,ambulances medical equipment rentals.
